## Kossy Langat Blueprint

Primary visitor question: Can Leon understand a full person and turn their identity, work, values, and leadership into a public-facing brand?

Business answer: Yes. CodeByLeon can translate a professional's role, character, values, work, mentorship, and credibility into a web presence that feels specific to the person.

Primary audience: professionals, consultants, founders, leaders, creators, and public-facing specialists who need a personal website that represents more than a resume.

CTA: `Build My Personal Brand` to `/get-started.html`.

Layout direction: editorial identity profile. Use narrative sections, large typographic moments, portrait-led website screenshots, character pillars, and proof moments. Avoid dashboard grids, funnel diagrams, and operations-heavy language.

## Delivah Dispatch Blueprint

Primary visitor question: Can Leon build a service-business website that gets customers in and gives operations a system behind the scenes?

Business answer: Yes. CodeByLeon can build a market-facing website connected to registration, document upload, backend storage, admin review, and customer contact flows.

Primary audience: service businesses, logistics operators, dispatch firms, and operations-heavy small businesses that need customer acquisition plus intake systems.

CTA: `Build My Service Funnel` to `/get-started.html`.

Layout direction: funnel-and-operations case study. Use a clear journey from visitor to services to intake to document upload to admin review. Avoid live business claims such as phone number, address, carrier count, licensing, or insurance status.
